About Us

SC .CARPATEX .SA Brasov is one of oldest and famous company in theRomanian wool fabric branch well known in the textile world.
The company was founded 1823 by SCHERG a german family, as the first textile mill in the Brasov area.
Gradually the company is constant developed and the range of fabrics increased therefor 1880 was the famous and modern textile firm in East Europe.
The wool fabrics were exported on European and Asian markets and worldwide recognises and appreciated as BRASOV CLOTH.
SC. CARPATEX .SA BRASOV, is a society integral private and has a strong economical potential with its modern technology and high qualifies employers.
The company has a complete production line - spinning, weaving and finishing wich  has modernized since 1990 and allows us to obtain the best fabric quality at international competitive standards.


 

We offer a complete fabric collection with design and fashionable colors and meet all market demands
The textile experience of generations is our guarantee for high quality fabrics required on major textile markets.
SC CARPATEX SA Brasov produces around :500.000 lm fabrics wool type, such as:

-super fine pure wool fabrics

-worsted fabrics (100% wool or wool/nylon for: women wears and men's wears suits

-carded fabrics (100%wool, cashmere wool/nylon for; coats, trousers skirts
